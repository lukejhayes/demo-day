Sales Representative Position

Sales Department

Alsip, Illinois - Rockford, Illinois

Job Position Purpose

The Merchandiser is a sales position responsible for directly promoting and servicing Hayes Beer Distributing Company's brands & programs to an assigned account base to achieve business objectives.

Accountability

The Sales Representative is accountable for results:
  • Sell, Sell, Sell
  • Execute pricing strategy by brand
  • Increase visibility for assigned brands
  • Manage account assortment & achieve brand distribution goals
  • Secure displays for assigned brands
  • Rotate product to ensure product FRESHNESS
  • Offer the BEST customer service: Stock all selling areas & build displays.
  • Execute in a timely manner all objectives
  • Develop TEAMWORK relationships across the organization
  • Look to exceed expectations
  • Participate in local events to promote sales (Special event schedule)

Sales Process

  1. Pre-plan the call
  2. Sign-in
  3. Walk the account
  4. Discover / identify opportunities
  5. Stock all selling areas / build displays / ensure that all product for sale meets package quality standards and is in code
  6. Merchandise with pos, ensure all prices are visible in all selling areas
  7. Plan execution & follow-up
  8. Take notes on objectives for the next call
