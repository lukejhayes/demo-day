Merchandiser

Position Purpose:

The Merchandiser is a sales support position responsible for directly promoting and servicing Hayes Beer Distributing Company's brands & programs to an assigned account base to achieve business objectives.

Accountability

The Merchandiser is accountable for results:
  • Execute pricing strategy by brand, ensure that price reflections are visible in all areas & on all SKU's.
  • Secure incremental displays for Hayes brands
  • Execute all pos initiatives
  • Execute all pos initiatives
  • Execute display racks
  • Reach know & impact account decision makers
  • Assist accounts in identifying & executing their top priorities for the beer category
  • Execute cooler sets & properly arrange brands to Hayes standards
  • Rotate product to ensure product FRESHNESS
  • Hang pos, ensure all pos materials hanging in the account is relevant & current theme
  • Offer the BEST customer service: Stock all selling areas & build displays
  • Ensure product appearance meets minimal standards
  • Help the retailer solve the damage issue
  • Lead by good example to the organization
  • Initiate tactics to impact sales
  • Provide competitive information to the Company
  • Provide input on current market evaluation
  • Execute in a timely manner all objectives
  • Develop TEAMWORK relationships across the organization
  • Look to exceed expectations
  • Participate in local events to promote sales (Special event schedule)

Sales Process

  1. Pre-plan the call
  2. Sign-in
  3. Walk the account
  4. Discover / identify opportunities
  5. Stock all selling areas / build displays / ensure that all product for sale meets package quality standards and is in code
  6. Merchandise with pos, ensure all prices are visible in all selling areas
  7. Prepare your presentation (Solution selling)
  8. Present to the retailer (Ask for what needs to get done!)
  9. Plan execution & follow-up
  10. Take notes on objectives for the next call
